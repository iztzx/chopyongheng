A Legacy of Fire and Flavor

From the bustling streets of Johor Bahru to your modern kitchen, Chop Yong Heng represents an unbroken chain of tradition. What began as a necessity in every household has evolved into a specialty craft.

Our charcoal doesn't just fuel your cooking—it enhances every dish with the subtle smokiness that gas and electric simply cannot match.
